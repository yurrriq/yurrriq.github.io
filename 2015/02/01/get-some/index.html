#'clj-util.get-some/get-some

01 February 2015

Sometimes I have a map with a number of different potential keys of interest, e.g. the :headers of a Ring request map. In such a case, I might want to get the value of the first of those keys that's present.

For this rare (but not quite edge) case, I wrote a simple loop to get some value of interest.

(defn get-some [m & ks]
  (loop [ks ks]
    (when (seq ks)
      (or (get m (first ks))
          (recur (rest ks))))))

;; Example usage
(get-some (:headers req)
  :content-type "content-type" :accept "accept")
